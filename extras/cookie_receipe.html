Very tasty cookies

This recipe describes how to make super tasty cookies that you can eat while programming your next webpage.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • HTML code

Preparation

  1. Put everything together, first milk and flour, than butter and eggs.
  2. Add some beautiful snippets of HTML code to make the cookies extra tasty.
  3. Stir until it looks nice. What "looks nice" means is up to you.
  4. Bake for exactly 42 minutes. Not longer and not shorter, this is very important.
  5. Don't eat them all up at once to avoid the HTML cookie sickness.
